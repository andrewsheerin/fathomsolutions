About SWPT

SWPT (Stormwater Pollution Tracker) helps municipalities understand, model, and reduce urban runoff pollution with optimized street sweeping strategies.

Stormwater Pollution

Pollutants such as sediment, metals, nutrients, and plastics wash into waterways during rain events.

Urban Runoff

Impervious surfaces increase flow volumes, carrying pollutants quickly into receiving waters.

Street Sweeping

One of the most cost-effective tools for removing pollutants before they reach storm drains.

Core Features

SWPT gives cities the tools to simulate pollutant loads, prioritize critical areas, and optimize strategies for maximum reduction.

Simulate

Model pollutant removal outcomes using different street sweeping schedules and strategies.

Prioritize

Identify critical road segments for pollutant capture using spatial and hydrologic insights.

Optimize

Generate sweeping plans that maximize pollution reduction while minimizing costs.
