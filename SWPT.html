Why Street Sweeping Matters

Urban streets accumulate excess pollutants such as sediment, metals, nutrients, and debris.

Stormwater runoff transports these pollutants directly into local waterways.

Street sweeping is the most cost-effective pollution control measure, but only when strategically scheduled and targeted.

What You Can Do with SWPT

Prioritize

Find the most efficient sweeper routes and cost-effective sweeping schedule for your sweeping program.
