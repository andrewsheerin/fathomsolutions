About Us

Hi, I’m Andrew Sheerin, founder of Fathom Solutions. I turn abstract ideas into tangible, technical solutions that bridge innovation and environmental impact. With a Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering and a background in systems design, I’ve shifted my focus toward entrepreneurship—building tools that make sustainability smarter. My work centers on environmental modeling for water and infrastructure systems, building products that deliver data insights and decision support.
